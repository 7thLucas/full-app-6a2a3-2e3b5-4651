Build a full Compliance Management App for Allied Financial Insurance that tracks employee compliance across multiple departments, initially focusing on Sales and Compliance/Risk. The app should allow employees to upload mandatory compliance documents, including regulatory trainings (AML, Anti-Fraud, Data Privacy), role-specific certifications (Sales License for Sales, CRCM for Compliance/Risk), signed SOP/compliance forms, and quiz or assessment results. Each document or input should have its own configurable rules: mandatory status, file type (PDF or numeric), passing score thresholds, expiration/renewal intervals, and escalation procedures. The system should automatically generate employee-level compliance scorecards showing completed, pending, and non-compliant items with color-coded status indicators and explanations. HR/Admin should have a dashboard showing overall compliance metrics, department-level summaries, top missing items, upcoming expirations, escalations, and the ability to send reminders or escalate items. Include automated tracking, audit logs, expiration alerts, and historical records. The app should be mobile-friendly and desktop-ready, with clear employee views showing their own compliance status and pending actions. Output should support CSV/PDF exports for audits, and the system must be flexible so HR can configure rules per department, per role, and per document. Provide the full app structure, including SOP mapping, scorecard logic, dashboards, document workflows, alerts, and escalation rules.
